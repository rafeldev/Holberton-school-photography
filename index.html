How was this passion born?

Soyraffaello arose from a change of perspective to life, he knew that life could not be seen as everyone used to see it but wanted to highlight those things or objects, which for others were simply objects, he saw anything or landscape in a way different, until he made the decision to start capturing all those images inside his mind and an old phone that barely only served to take photos and browse social networks, just there that July 11, 2016 began this whole story about soyraffaello. A boy who follows behind those things that are almost invisible to other people, that's how I feel, that's how I am, here and now.

Why one day I decided to start taking pictures?

I have told my personal story with the photo countless times, surely you know that I started taking pictures after seeing my father with an SLR when we were going on vacation. But there is a specific place that made me feel so much that it was then that I knew that I should take the path of photography. Those landscapes captivated me so much that I said "I want to spend my whole life taking pictures in places as amazing as this." I saw it so clearly, that a year later I started studying Image. And so it all triggered. Until today.
